Types of E-waste to recycle

We only accept regulated E-Waste. Non-regulated E-Waste are non-regulated under the Extended Producer Responsibility Scheme for E-Waste Management. We recommend for you to bring your E-Waste down to the Cash for Trash (CFT) station.
If you are still unsure if your E-Waste is recyclable, do use our E-Waste scanner and we will tell you if it can be recycled.

Here is a list of regulated E-Waste:

  • Large Household Appliances
  • Electric Mobility Devices
  • House Hold Batteries
  • Lithium Ion Portable Batteries
  • Consumer Electirc Vehical Batteries
  • Consumer Lamp
  • ICT Equipment

Find out more on our Education Page or in the ALBA E-Waste website!
